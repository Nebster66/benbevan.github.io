About Me

I’m a computer scientist turned game developer and designer based in South Wales, currently Product Owner at Wee Tattie Studios. My passion for gaming drives me to create my own games and pursue a career in the industry!

I’ve been playing games for as long as I can remember — starting on the PS One and working through a variety of platforms. My curiosity for how games are made began when I bought my first PC at 11 and started creating games in Scratch and Kodu.

That passion returned during university, where I taught myself Unreal Engine alongside my Computer Science studies and even used it in my dissertation and cybersecurity research role. After graduating, I joined the volunteer-driven Wee Tattie Studios as Lead Designer and Product Owner.

In my personal projects, I love experimenting with ideas that combine mechanics from multiple favourite games into something new and unique.
