Planet Looper

An arcade-style one-button game made for the GMTK Game Jam 2025. Built solo in Godot 4 within 96 hours, focusing on minimalism, tight loops, and replayability.

Gameplay Concept

The core mechanic was inspired by circular movement puzzles. The game’s simple control scheme hides complex timing challenges and score multipliers.

Design Iteration

Several iterations were tested for colour contrast, orbit spacing, and feedback timing to ensure an intuitive yet challenging rhythm to gameplay.

Environment & Art

A clean, minimal UI style was chosen to emphasise focus and flow.

Game Design Breakdown

Core Loop

Players complete rotation cycles around planets, timing inputs to collect orbits and avoid obstacles. This formed the heartbeat of the gameplay experience.

Level Design

Each level was built around rhythm and difficulty scaling, focusing on minimal cognitive load with increasing precision demands.
